## Daily Routine Checklist

### Morning (9:00 AM - 15 min)
- [ ] Check Stripe Dashboard for disputes
- [ ] Scan inbox, flag P0/P1 issues
- [ ] Respond to any P0 immediately
- [ ] Batch respond to P2 with macros

### Evening (6:00 PM - 15 min)
- [ ] Follow up on morning P1 issues
- [ ] Process remaining P2 tickets
- [ ] Quick check on app store reviews
- [ ] Note any patterns for improvement

### Weekly (Friday - 30 min)
- [ ] Review ticket volume by category
- [ ] Check chargeback rate (keep under 0.75%)
- [ ] Identify if new macros needed
- [ ] Update docs if patterns emerge

## Priority Quick Reference

| Priority | Response Time | Examples |
|----------|---------------|----------|
| **P0** | 2 hours | Safety, fraud, chargebacks, service down |
| **P1** | 4 hours | Payment failed, barber no-show, account locked |
| **P2** | 24 hours | Everything else |

---

## Key Metrics to Watch

| Metric | Target | Red Flag |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Chargeback rate | < 0.5% | > 0.75% |
| First response time | < 4 hours | > 24 hours |
| Refund rate | < 3% | > 5% |

## Important Terminology

**Use:** "Identity verification" - Confirms barbers are who they claim to be
**Never use:** "Background check" - We don't do these

## Escalation Quick Guide

| Situation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Safety threat | SAFETY_ESCALATION.md - drop everything |
| Chargeback | REFUND_DISPUTE_FLOW.md - respond in 3 days |
| No-show | NO_SHOW_HANDLING.md - follow process |
| Legal threat | Document, don't engage on substance, contact lawyer |
| Unsure | When in doubt, escalate up |
