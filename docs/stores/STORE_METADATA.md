## 3. Full Description

### Google Play / App Store (4000 characters max)

**Current Length:** ~2,800 characters

```
Direct Cuts connects you with skilled, identity-verified barbers in your area. Whether you need a fresh fade, a classic cut, or a beard trim, find the perfect barber and book instantly.

WHY DIRECT CUTS?

VERIFIED BARBERS
Every barber on Direct Cuts completes our identity verification process. Know who you're booking with and feel confident in your choice.

REAL-TIME AVAILABILITY
See exactly when barbers are available. No more back-and-forth messaging - just tap to book a time that works for you.

TRANSPARENT PRICING
All prices are shown upfront. No surprises, no hidden fees. What you see is what you pay.

GENUINE REVIEWS
Read honest reviews from real customers. Our verified review system helps you find barbers with proven track records.

SECURE PAYMENTS
Pay securely through the app with saved payment methods. Tips and payments are processed safely and quickly.

FEATURES FOR CLIENTS:
- Search barbers by location, specialty, and availability
- View portfolios of past work
- Book appointments in seconds
- Get reminders before your appointment
- Leave reviews to help the community
- Save favorite barbers for quick rebooking
- In-app messaging with your barber

FEATURES FOR BARBERS:
- Build your professional profile
- Set your own schedule and prices
- Accept bookings 24/7
- Get paid directly to your account
- Grow your client base organically
- Manage your calendar in one place
- Track your earnings and reviews

GETTING STARTED:
1. Create your free account
2. Allow location access to find nearby barbers
3. Browse profiles and reviews
4. Book your appointment
5. Show up and get a great cut!

CONTACT & SUPPORT:
Questions? Reach us at support@direct-cuts.com

We're committed to connecting clients with talented barbers while providing a safe, transparent booking experience for everyone.

Download Direct Cuts today and discover your next favorite barber.
```

### Key Messaging Guidelines

**ALWAYS USE:**
- "Identity-verified barbers"
- "Verified professionals"
- "Identity verification process"
- "Verified reviews"
- "Secure payments"

**NEVER USE:**
- "Background check" or "background checked"
- "Background screening"
- "Criminal check"
- "Vetted" (ambiguous connotation)
- "Pre-screened" (implies more than identity verification)

## 7. Screenshot Guidelines

### Content Requirements

Each screenshot should highlight a key feature:

| Screenshot # | Content | Key Message |
|--------------|---------|-------------|
| 1 | Map/Discovery | "Find barbers near you" |
| 2 | Barber Profile | "Verified professionals" |
| 3 | Booking Flow | "Book in seconds" |
| 4 | Reviews | "Real customer reviews" |
| 5 | Payment | "Secure, easy payment" |
| 6 | Confirmation | "You're all set!" |

### Text Overlay Guidelines

**DO:**
- Keep text minimal (3-6 words)
- Use app's brand colors
- Ensure readability on all backgrounds
- Show actual app UI

**DON'T:**
- Include "background check" language
- Use generic stock photos
- Overcrowd with text
- Show placeholder content

| Screenshot | Overlay Text |
